Two locations one museum

A platform that reflects the contemporary and classic duality of the museum.

Frans Hals Museum
Frans Hals Museum and KesselsKramer commissioned us to create a platform that reflects the contemporary and classic duality of the museum.
Awwwards Site of the Day, FWA Site of the Day, CSS Design Award Design, development www.franshalsmuseum.nl

On 29 March 2018, the Frans Hals Museum, with its outstanding collection of Old Masters, ‘marries’ De Hallen Haarlem, the museum for contemporary and modern art in Haarlem.

KesselsKramer main concept was to combine the old with the new in a mirror. This concept is the main theme that we used throughout the website.

The identity has been designed by KesselsKramer. We expanded the visual style in the platform.

The Identity is translated in an online environment.

The website has a playful touch which makes it usable for every target audience.

We created a custom icon set that mirrors the signage of the museum.

We created a toolset so the museum can explain their art pieces with fine details.
